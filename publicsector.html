Public Sector Solutions

Our Learning and Capacity Development training solutions to hundreds of public sector officials in various state government ministries, Departments and Agencies have led to an increase in performance, communication, MDAs growth and restructuring across the board, improved public sector practices and excellent service delivery.

Some of our public sector solutions include:

  • Training Offerings for Public Sector Officials and Civil Servants in MDAs

  • Public Sector Project Management (Retreats, Conferences, Workshops, etc.)

  • Business Advisory & Professional Consulting Services for MDAs

  • Procurement Services.

  • Coaching/FLO Training Certification Program for Public Sector Officials


EZ37 Solutions, with RC Number 833803 established in 2009, specialises in delivering diverse Coaching and Learning and Development programs that inspire employees to be more engaged and motivated.
