Facilitating Learning - Online & In-Person

The International Finance Corporation – Learning Performance Institute (IFC-LPI) course draws from IFC’s Principles of Learning and is delivered via a network of regional Master Trainers and models best practice behaviours and skills.


Upon completion of this course, participants qualify to apply for IFC-LPI’s Trainer Performance Monitoring and Assessment (TPMA), an internationally recognized trainer certification.

This virtual programme incorporates the curriculum of the International Finance Corporation – Learning and Performance Institute (IFC-LPI). Discover strategies to effectively communicate information, captivate your audience and create engaging learning experiences online and in-person.

As a result, participants experience heightened engagement and achieve improved learning outcomes.

EZ37 Solutions, with RC Number 833803 established in 2009, specialises in delivering diverse Coaching and Learning and Development programs that inspire employees to be more engaged and motivated.
