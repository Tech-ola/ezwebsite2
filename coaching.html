Our Coaching Offerings

Coaching Skills for Leaders

The coaching skills for leaders and managers training will equip leaders and managers with knowledge of coaching and the mindset required for effective coaching in the workplace. This will enable them to demonstrate behaviours that support their team members to begin to take ownership of their development and maximise their potential.


Group/Team Coaching

Group/Team coaching uses an approach that partners with the team/group in creating an effective thinking environment. It nurtures the cross-fertilization of ideas and offers several perspectives among the team/group. The process is facilitated by coaches that are credentialed by the International Coaching Federation (ICF).


One-to-One Executive Coaching
